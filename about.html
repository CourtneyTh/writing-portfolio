About

Hello and welcome to my writing portfolio. My name is Courtney and I've been writing across a variety of topics and formats for about 8 years. Professionally, I have worked on marketing campaigns to deliver engaging and well-performing pieces for my clients' websites and blogs. Much of my personal time is also spent writing and I enjoy any project I can get swept up in, from short stories and screenplays to personal essays and travel guides.

My portfolio is a culmination of the work I've done over the years and will continue to grow. Please explore the different categories to get a sense of my writing style and full body of work.
