Gifts from Henry

FADE IN:

EXT. APARTMENT BUILDING - EVENING

HENRY (20's) waits at the entrance of the building, holding a red HEART-SHAPED BOX of chocolates. His hair is gelled back and he wears a pair of jeans with loafers and a sport coat. He clutches the box and taps his fingers on it.

The door opens and a young woman, RACHEL (20's) walks out. She is dressed casually, in leggings and a coat. She rolls her eyes at Henry.

Henry holds the box out in front of him.

HENRY

I know I screwed up, Rachel. I shouldn't have forgot our date. I'm sorry.

Rachel's gaze softens. She looks at the box and back at Henry. Moving closer to him, she places a hand the box and raises an eyebrow.

RACHEL

Promise it won't happen again?

HENRY

Promise.

Rachel smiles and leans in. They kiss and she takes the box.

INT. APARTMENT LIVING ROOM - NIGHT

The door opens and a LIGHT turns on.

Henry and Rachel walk inside, LAUGHING.

They take off their coats and Henry hangs both on a chair in the kitchen. He puts a brown paper bag in the fridge. Rachel holds the box of chocolates.

HENRY

So it's decided.

He walks into the living room and grabs the remote, turning on Netflix.

HENRY(CONT'D)

The Princess Bride. For the-

(looks off in thought)

third time.

Henry sits on the couch and Rachel tilts her head, raising her eyebrows.

RACHEL

It's a classic.

She waves the box.

RACHEL(CONT'D)

Like chocolate.

Henry jumps from the couch and rushes to the kitchen. He takes the box from Rachel.

HENRY

I'm still pretty full. Why don't we save those for later?

RACHEL

I'm sure you have room for at least one.

Rachel TUGS the box from Henry's grip. She gives him a look under furrowed brows and opens the box.

It's EMPTY.

Henry GRIMACES and looks down. Rachel turns to him with wide eyes and an open mouth.

Henry holds up his hands.

HENRY

Okay, see-

RACHEL

What the hell is this?

HENRY

To be fair, you said you were cutting back on sugar.

Rachel SLAMS the box on the table.

RACHEL

What?

Henry runs his hand through his hair and shrugs.

HENRY

(muttering)

I didn't have time to shop. I figured you wouldn't open it for a while.

Rachel scoffs.

RACHEL

You gave me your trash? And what was supposed to happen here, we could fight whenever I did open it?

HENRY

You always complain about your roommate eating your food so I thought...

Rachel shoves him with both hands and grabs her coat from the chair.

RACHEL

You are unbelievable.

Henry stands in front of her.

HENRY

So I'm not the best at gifts. Is that really the worst thing?

Rachel puts on her coat and pushes Henry aside.

RACHEL

Not the best? You got me "diamond" earrings without the diamonds for Valentine's Day! They're just empty settings! Who the hell is that cheap?

HENRY

Well... I stand by what I said! The diamond industry is corrupt. Those are a great statement piece.

Rachel squeezes her eyes shut and brings her hands to her temples.

RACHEL

That's not what that means!

Rachel marches to the door and turns back to Henry, shaking her head.

RACHEL

Your stupidity is just... inconceivable! Enjoy the rest of your date night without a date, okay?

She opens the door and storms out, coat trailing behind her.

Henry stands halfway to the door, mouth agape.

HENRY

Fine!

He snatches the crumpled box from the table and charges to the door. He waves the box in front of him.

HENRY

(shouting)

You know what? Maybe this box is a statement piece for how empty your heart is!

Henry throws the box down the hall. His eyes well up and he sighs.

HENRY

(shouting)

Wait Rachel, I didn't mean it!

He races down the hallway and disappears around the corner.

FADE OUT.
